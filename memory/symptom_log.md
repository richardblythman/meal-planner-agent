# Symptom Log

Track symptoms to identify patterns and potential food triggers.

---

## How to Log

Add entries below with:
- **Date & Time**
- **Bristol Scale** (1-7) - gut symptoms
- **Eczema** (0-3: 0=clear, 1=mild, 2=moderate, 3=severe) + location
- **Ears** (0-3: 0=fine, 1=itchy/full, 2=irritated, 3=infected)
- **Meals in preceding 24-48 hours**
- **Notes** (stress, sleep, anything relevant)

---

## Common Triggers to Watch

| Food | Associated With |
|------|-----------------|
| Dairy | Eczema, gut issues, mucus/congestion |
| Gluten | Eczema, gut issues |
| Eggs | Eczema |
| Sugar | Inflammation, gut issues |
| Alcohol | Eczema flares, gut irritation |
| Nightshades | Inflammation (tomatoes, peppers, potatoes) |
