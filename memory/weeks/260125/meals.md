## Meal Plan

### Day 1 - Monday
**Dinner:** [Crispy Potato and Smoked Salmon Power Bowls](../../recipes/crispy-potato-and-smoked-salmon-power-bowls.md)

### Day 2 Lunch (Wednesday)
**Leftovers:** Blackened Chicken Taco Bowls from Day 2 Dinner

### Day 3 - Wednesday
**Dinner:** [Seabass Bowls with Caramelized Onions and Warm Tomatoes](../../recipes/seabass-bowls-with-caramelized-onions-and-warm-tomatoes.md)

Pan-seared seabass over quinoa with Foster sauce (caramelized onions and warm tomatoes), hard-boiled eggs, avocado, and dukkah.

### Day 3 Lunch (Thursday)
**Leftovers:** Seabass Bowls with Caramelized Onions from Day 3 Dinner

---

### Day 4 - Thursday
**Dinner:** Roast Chicken with Sweet Potatoes and Parsnips (Component Meal)

Roasted whole chicken with roasted sweet potatoes and parsnips. Simple, comforting, and nutritious.

**Components:**
- [Whole Chicken](../../components/proteins/whole-chicken.md) - 250g cooked meat per serving
- Sweet Potatoes (fresh) - 200g per serving
- Parsnips (fresh) - 150g per serving
- Olive oil for roasting - 15ml per serving

### Day 4 Lunch (Friday)
**Leftovers:** Roast Chicken with Sweet Potatoes and Parsnips from Day 4 Dinner
