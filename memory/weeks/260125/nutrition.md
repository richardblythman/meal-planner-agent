## Preparation Notes

### Meal Prep Tips

**Day 1 (Monday) - Crispy Potato Salmon Bowls:**
- Roast potatoes and onions first (25 min)
- Bake salmon while potatoes finish (12-15 min)
- Hard-boil eggs ahead of time
- Make kefir sauce in advance (keeps 3-4 days)

**Day 2 (Tuesday) - Blackened Chicken Taco Bowls:**
- Cook rice while oven preheats
- Bake chicken (20-25 min)
- Make pico de gallo and guacamole fresh
- Dressing can be made ahead

**Day 3 (Wednesday) - Seabass Bowls:**
- Cook quinoa (250g total, split 2:1 - you get 167g dry, partner gets 83g dry) (15 min + 5 min steam)
- Make Foster Sauce while quinoa cooks
- Sear seabass last (4-6 min total)
- Hard-boiled eggs can be prepped with Day 1 batch

**Day 4 (Thursday) - Roast Chicken:**
- Allow 1.5-2 hours for whole chicken roasting
- Roast sweet potatoes and parsnips alongside chicken (last 45 min)
- Rest chicken 10-15 min before carving

### Storage Reminders
- All meals designed for next-day lunch leftovers
- Store components separately where possible
- Fresh items (avocado, guacamole) best added day-of
- Hard-boiled eggs keep 5 days refrigerated
