## Recommendation

**DIY Essential stack is the sweet spot:**
- Saves ~€350-540/year vs AG1
- Covers nutrients AG1 is actually missing (Vitamin D, adequate Magnesium)
- Skips the proprietary blends with unknown dosages
- More control over individual dosages

**AG1's main value proposition** is convenience (one scoop) and the greens/adaptogens blend - but you don't know if those are at therapeutic doses.
