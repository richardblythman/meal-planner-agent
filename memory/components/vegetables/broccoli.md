# Broccoli

**Category**: Vegetable
**Prep Time**: 5 minutes
**Cook Time**: 5-10 minutes
**Serving Size**: 150g (cooked)

## Nutrition per Serving
- Calories: 52
- Protein: 4g
- Carbs: 10g
- Fat: 0.5g
- Fiber: 4g
- Vitamin C: 135% DV
- Vitamin K: 180% DV

## Common Cooking Methods
- Steamed (5-7 min)
- Roasted (400°F, 15-20 min with oil)
- Sautéed (5-7 min)
- Raw

## Notes
- Best paired with: chicken, salmon, beef, rice
- Keeps 4-5 days refrigerated
- Roasting brings out sweetness
