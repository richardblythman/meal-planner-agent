# Kiwi

**Category**: Fruit
**Prep Time**: 2 minutes
**Cook Time**: 0 minutes (eaten raw)
**Serving Size**: 1 kiwi (~65g)

## Nutrition per Serving
- Calories: 36
- Protein: 0.7g
- Carbs: 6.9g
- Fat: 0.3g
- Fiber: 1.2g
- Sugars: 6.7g
- Vitamin C: 38mg
- Salt: <0.01g

## Nutrition per 100g
- Calories: 55
- Protein: 1.1g
- Carbs: 10.6g
- Fat: 0.5g
- Fiber: 1.9g
- Sugars: 10.3g
- Vitamin C: 59mg

## Common Preparation Methods
- Halved and scooped with spoon
- Peeled and sliced
- Diced for fruit salads
- Blended into smoothies

## Notes
- Best paired with: yogurt, oatmeal, other fruits, smoothie bowls
- Ripen at room temperature, then refrigerate
- High in Vitamin C (excellent immune support)
- Store unripe kiwis at room temperature until soft
- Refrigerated ripe kiwis keep 1-2 weeks
