## Common Preparation Methods
- Peeled and eaten whole
- Sliced for cereal, oatmeal, or yogurt
- Mashed for baking or smoothies
- Frozen for smoothies or nice cream

## Notes
- Best paired with: oatmeal, yogurt, peanut butter, smoothies, cereal
- Good source of potassium and natural energy
- Ripen at room temperature; refrigerate once ripe (skin darkens but flesh stays fresh)
- Overripe bananas ideal for baking or freezing
- Store away from other fruits to prevent premature ripening
