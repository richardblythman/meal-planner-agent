## Cooking Instructions
1. Rinse rice under cold water until water runs clear
2. Use 1.5 cups water per 1 cup rice
3. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 12-15 minutes
4. Remove from heat, let steam covered for 5 minutes
5. Fluff with fork before serving

## Notes
- Long-grain aromatic rice with distinct nutty flavour
- Lower glycemic index than other white rice varieties
- Pairs well with curries, stir-fries, and bowls
- Storage: Keep in airtight container
