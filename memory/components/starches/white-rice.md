# White Rice

**Category**: Starch
**Prep Time**: 1 minute
**Cook Time**: 15-20 minutes
**Serving Size**: 150g (cooked)

## Nutrition per Serving
- Calories: 195
- Protein: 4g
- Carbs: 43g
- Fat: 0.4g
- Fiber: 0.5g

## Common Cooking Methods
- Stovetop (1:1.5 rice to water ratio)
- Rice cooker
- Instant pot (4 min pressure + natural release)

## Notes
- Best paired with: any protein, stir fry vegetables, curries
- Batch cook for the week
- Keeps 4-5 days refrigerated
- Reheat with splash of water
